# CPFEH

Crystal Plasticity Finite Element Homogenization model for
[Dyalog APL](https://www.dyalog.com/)

Example:

    eg.apls

Expected output:

            50         10       0.05      2.783
           100         10       0.05      4.358
           150         10       0.05      8.288
           200         10       0.05     12.661
           250         10       0.05     21.864
            50       1000       0.05    626.796  r.csv  ea.csv  f.csv

Timings (in the fourth column) may vary.
